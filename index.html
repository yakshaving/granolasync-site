GranolaSync

Automatically sync your Granola meeting notes and action items to Planka boards and Linear issues.

Download for macOS

macOS 13+ · Apple Silicon & Intel · Free

📋

Meeting Notes Sync

Pulls your latest meetings from Granola and converts ProseMirror content into clean Markdown for your boards.

⚡

Action Item Triage

Extracts action items from meeting notes. Review, assign, and push them to Planka or Linear in one click.

🛠

Menubar Native

Lives in your macOS menubar. Always one click away, never in your way. No Electron, no browser — pure SwiftUI.

Get Started

  1. Download the .dmg above and drag GranolaSync to Applications.
  2. Open GranolaSync — it appears in your menubar.
  3. Open Settings and enter your Planka or Linear credentials.
  4. Click Refresh to pull meetings, then triage action items to your boards.
